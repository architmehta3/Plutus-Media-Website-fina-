About Plutus Media

PLUTUS MEDIA is a music publisher and a record label also specializes into royalty management and music rights clearances for synchronization, mechanical, adaptation, sheet etc.

Plutus Media draws on the expertise of professionals, fellows of radio, television, Mobile, internet etc. We work proactively to guarantee you peace of mind so you can focus on what’s important – your music.

As a composer, you are busy and often more worried about making that deadline rather than tracking broadcasts and ensuring that your royalty payments are accurate. Did you know you only have three years to file a claim for royalties, which may not have been paid?

As a publisher, you may not have the knowledge base or the manpower in your company to monitor your international broadcasts and keep up to date on the latest copyright news and technology. We can help!

Our Mission

  1. Plutus Media delivers a time efficient and cost effective creative music solution for production houses, Advertising Agencies, TV, Film & Gaming production companies around the world.

  2. We provide Creatives, Directors, Producers & Editors with a ‘One Stop Shop’ solution, be it creative music searches, rights clearance & negotiation & licensing, pre-cleared music, bespoke composition & sound design, re-records etc.

  3. Plutus Media also provides Artists, Composers & Producers with a global platform and opportunity for your music to be placed in Films, TV, Advertising, Games & all other forms of media.

  4. We are the music experts and we combine this knowledge with some of the best creative agencies in the business with an aspiration to produce outstanding work.

Publishing

Royalty Collection & Administration

Over and above anything else, we attach great importance to the efficient collection of royalties for our songwriters and publishers. At Plutus Media, our job is to know where, when and by whom, song titles have been released and performed. Our system allows us to proactively claim all revenue accrued, uncollected and / or underpaid.

Sub-Publishers

All our copyrights are properly represented overseas by some of the best independent music publishers around. Maintaining constant and strong communication with our colleagues around the world on both a creative and administrative basis is how we keep on top of our writers publishing needs across the globe.
In addition to our own catalogue, the company also administers a growing number of third party music publishing companies.

We are flexible & respond to the needs of writers

As independent publishers, we are able to be completely flexible and respond to the needs of today’s writers. Our simple administration agreements offer all the advantages of publishing and co-publishing arrangements without our writers having to surrender their artistic rights.
We aim to build transparent and trusting partnerships with our writers and to give them the personal attention that they deserve, a service that major publishing companies and even larger independent publishers are simply unable to offer.
